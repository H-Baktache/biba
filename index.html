Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how our Job Platform ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services that connect job publishers and job seekers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information to provide our job platform services:

  • Profile Information: Full name, profile picture, phone number, and email address
  • Professional Information: Work experience, education, skills, and qualifications
  • CV/Resume Data: Content, templates, and documents you create or upload
  • Job Preferences: Desired positions, salary expectations, location preferences, and industry interests
  • Account Information: Login credentials and account settings

1.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your use of our platform:

  • Job search activities and application history
  • CV creation and editing activities
  • Communication with job publishers and other users
  • Platform preferences and settings
  • Device information and app performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and maintain your professional profile and CV
  • To match you with relevant job opportunities from publishers
  • To facilitate communication between job seekers and job publishers
  • To provide personalized job recommendations and career insights
  • To improve our platform functionality and user experience
  • To send notifications about job opportunities, messages, and platform updates
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To ensure platform security and prevent fraud

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Job Publishers: Your profile information, CV, and professional details may be shared with job publishers when you apply for positions or when publishers search for candidates
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties when you explicitly authorize us to do so
  • Service Providers: We work with trusted third-party service providers who assist in platform operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information to comply with legal obligations or protect our rights and safety

4. Data Security and Storage

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
  • Secure Storage: Your data is stored in secure, monitored facilities with access controls
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security assessments and updates
  • Access Controls: Strict authentication and authorization measures protect your data
  • Data Backup: Regular backups ensure your information is protected against loss

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: View and download your personal data and CV information
  • Update: Modify your profile information, CV, and preferences at any time
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Control Visibility: Manage who can see your profile and CV information
  • Communication Preferences: Opt-out of certain notifications and communications
  • Data Portability: Export your data in a standard format

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our job platform services to you
  • Maintain your professional profile and CV
  • Facilitate job applications and communications
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

You may request deletion of your data at any time through the app settings or by contacting us directly.

7. Third-Party Services

Our platform may integrate with third-party services for:

  • User authentication and account management
  • File storage and document processing
  • Payment processing (if applicable)
  • Analytics and platform performance monitoring
  • Communication and notification services

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

8. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during such transfers, including compliance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Sending you a notification through the platform
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
